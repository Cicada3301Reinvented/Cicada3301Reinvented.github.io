We are individuals who come together and share common interest putting our ideas into existence in order to contribute to the Open source Community. We try to make ‘I don’t know’ to ‘I don’t know yet’. Many people have genuine ideas related to App and Web Development but there is no proper platform provided to them. We, as MSTC, come here and help them give the right environment to inculcate their ideas and Develop. We aim to do this in as exciting and simplified manner as possible. We help people find answers to their questions, share ideas, solve problems and help them get more out of their ideas and technology. We also come up with various events and competitions which help them to create a strong foundation for their dream competitions like GSoC, Outreachy, Imagine Cup, etc.
